Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Budget Beacon. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you link your Apple Card transactions within our iOS app.

2. Data We Collect

When you choose to link your Apple Card in Budget Beacon, we collect:

  • Transaction dates and amounts
  • Merchant names and categories
  • Account balances and spending summaries

All data is retrieved directly from Apple’s secure APIs; we do not collect any banking credentials or card numbers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your transaction data solely to:

  • Generate monthly budget overviews
  • Detect and highlight spending spikes
  • Track spending by store or category
  • Provide insights through our intuitive interface

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing or advertising.

4. Data Storage & Security

All transaction data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption (AES-256). We follow Apple’s best practices for secure storage on device. Only you can view your financial information, and it remains on your iPhone unless you explicitly export it.

5. Third-Party Services

We rely on Apple’s Wallet and Card APIs to access your transaction history. Budget Beacon does not share your data with any other external services.

6. Your Choices

  • You can revoke Budget Beacon’s access to your Apple Card transactions at any time via your iOS Settings.
  • Unlinking your card will immediately delete locally stored transaction data within the app.

7. Children’s Privacy

Budget Beacon is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via an in-app alert or push notification.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@budgetbeacon.app.
